About SimER

SimER is an AI-driven simulation environment to assist call-taker training under emergency response scenarios.

What SimER Can Do

SimER currently supports the following major functionalities:

  • Simulation with Generative AI: SimER tailors the most up-to-date LLMs as its backend agents with a sophisticatedly designed prompt generation algorithm to generate realistic, valid, and equitable calls.
  • Conversation Reconstruction: At the end of each simulation, SimER reconstructs the past conversation and supports replay features (in both transcribed text and original audios).
  • Performance Debriefing: SimER debriefs the past simulation and offers advice to the user for future improvements. SimER also records feedback from users and iteratively refines the system with the local dispatching center.
